Description

With a built-in timer, heat meter, and jump counter, our wireless jump rope is the perfect tool for effective workouts to help you improve your fitness and maintain your health. Thanks to advanced technology and reliable materials, this jump rope will become your indispensable assistant in achieving your fitness goals and maintaining an optimal level of physical activity.

One of the main advantages of our wireless jump rope is its built-in timer, which allows you to accurately track the duration of your workouts. You can set the timer to the desired time and concentrate on achieving maximum results without wasting extra time on counting.

In addition, our skipping rope is equipped with a heat meter that measures the number of calories burned during your workout. This allows you to monitor your physical activity and provides an accurate analysis of your energy intake, which helps you achieve the desired results in weight loss or fitness.

Our wireless skipping rope is easy to use and maneuverable. It does not require a power connection or additional cables, making it the perfect choice for working out at home, in the gym or outdoors. 

Our wireless skipping rope is designed for anyone who values an active lifestyle and strives to maintain an optimal level of physical activity.  Choose our wireless skipping rope and enjoy an effective workout that will bring you pleasure and benefit your health!
